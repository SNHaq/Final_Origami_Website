About My Website!

This website was designed to help teach people the art of origami! Many people believe that origami is very complex and hard, however, that isn't true. Origami is actually quite simple, and is extremely relaxing. In this website I have listed several of my favorite origami animals to fold. I hope that you find this website useful and develop a passion for origami!

Why I Created This Website

When I was first starting origami, I thought that it would be extremely hard, but I was extremely wrong! Thanks to tutorials like these, I was able to pick it up really fast! I hope that my website helps others develop a newfound passion for origami! I created this website so that I could share and express my passion for origami! I also created this website to help introduce more people to the art of orgami.
